How to Use the Syndicate Chatbot

Log in first: +

The Syndicate Chatbot is designed exclusively for residents of our smart building. Log in with your credentials to access personalized assistance.

Is it safe to use the Syndicate Chatbot? +

Your safety and the security of your information are our top priorities. Rest assured, your data is protected, and no unauthorized access is allowed.

What if I ask something not related to the building? +

The chatbot is programmed to handle a variety of questions beyond building-related queries, ensuring you have the best possible experience.

What's the feedback form for? +

The feedback form allows residents to share their experiences, suggest improvements, and rate their overall satisfaction with our services.

How many languages does the Syndicate support? +

Currently, the Syndicate primarily operates in English. However, for important house-related queries, it can also respond in Arabic and French based on the user's preference.


Important Questions

How do I check the temperature in my house? +

Simply ask the chatbot, "What's the temperature in my house?" and it will provide you with the latest reading.

What should I do if I detect unusual activity? +

If you notice any unusual activity, immediately contact the building security team and follow their instructions.

How do I report equipment issues? +

The Syndicate will provide you with the contact information of a technician who can assist you.

How do I reset utilities? +

The Syndicate will guide you through resetting utilities manually if it's straightforward. For more complex issues, it will connect you with a technician.

How do I reserve spaces or check the garbage schedule? +

You can ask the chatbot about garbage schedules, shared spaces, and reservations for common areas.
